    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is CoinTower?

    CoinTower is a cryptocurrency banking website that allows you to buy, sell, and hold various cryptocurrencies.

    How do I create an account?

    You can create an account by visiting our login page and following the on-screen instructions.

    How do I add funds to my account?

    You can add funds to your account by linking a bank account or credit card and initiating a deposit.

    How do I buy cryptocurrency?

    You can buy cryptocurrency by navigating to the "Buy" tab in the website and selecting the cryptocurrency you wish to purchase.

    How do I sell cryptocurrency?

    You can sell cryptocurrency by navigating to the "Sell" tab in the website and selecting the cryptocurrency you wish to sell.

    How do I convert funds from my account?

    You can convert funds from your account by navigating to the "Convert" tab in the website and selecting the withdrawal method.

    How do I withdraw funds from my account?

    You can withdraw funds from your account by navigating to the "Withdraw" tab in the website and selecting the withdrawal method.

    Is CoinTower secure?

    Yes, CoinTower uses industry-leading security measures to protect your account and funds.

    How do I contact customer support?

    You can contact customer support by navigating to the "Company" tab in the app and selecting "Contact Us".
